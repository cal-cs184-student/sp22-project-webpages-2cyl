Summary of Accomplishments

We were unable to meet our goals of implementing a fluid simulator using Material Point Method due to difficulties with converting the Cloth Simulator into a Fluid Simulator.

For instance, we found that rendering spheres to be inefficient as just 10,000 would lag our computers. A fix to this is to use triangles instead of spheres. However, the triangles are flat. To give the illusion of volume, we will have to make the triangles face the camera.

OpenGL also has a bit of a learning curve that we did not expect. For project 4, most of the OpenGL was abstracted away but we have to directly interact with it here.

We have successfully managed to render a point cloud of triangles with a basic gravitational force. The physics itself and MPM have not yet been implemented and we hope to do so by next week.




Updated Goals

We may have to update our stretch goals such as incorporating different parallelization techniques since we are a bit behind schedule. Fluid solid coupling and multiple fluid simulations are also more difficult than we expected.
